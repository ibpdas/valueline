Executive decision box LEADING
Key trade offs and choices for senior leaders in this period, informed by Levels 1, 2 and 3.
Trade offs to manage now
• Balance investment between business as usual remediation and new capabilities.
• Prioritise cross cutting data standards versus urgent operational delivery pressures.
• Allocate analytical capacity between regulatory, policy and operational needs.
• Decide level of ambition for automation versus manual data collection approaches.
Top 3 asks from the board
• Approve investment choice between foundational remediation and new capability development.
• Confirm cross departmental adoption of shared data standards for key domains.
• Endorse sequencing for data ownership and stewardship rollout across ALBs and directorates.
Key dependencies this period
• Digital platform migration and reference data integration remain on-track.
• Directorate and ALB engagement sustained for ownership model adoption.
• HR approval for next wave of data and AI literacy uplift.
Confidence in evidence
• Level 1: High - based on delivery artefacts, project trackers and governance papers.
• Level 2: Medium - maturity inputs influenced by survey cycle and self assessment quality.
• Level 3: Medium to low - benefits realisation evidence still emerging and lagging behind activity.
